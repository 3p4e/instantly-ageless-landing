## 7. Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO)

### A/B Test Ideas
1. **Headline variations**: "Look 10 Years Younger" vs. "Facelift Without Surgery" vs. "Wrinkles Gone in 2 Minutes"
2. **CTA button color and text**: "Get Instant Results" vs. "Claim Your Discount" vs. "See Before & Afters"
3. **Video vs. static hero image**
4. **Pricing table design**: Highlighted "Best Value" vs. simple list
5. **FAQ placement**: Above the fold vs. end of page
6. **Urgency elements**: Countdown timers vs. social proof counters
7. **Testimonial format**: Text quotes vs. video testimonials

### Email Capture Optimization
- Test exit‑intent popup timing
- Offer: "10% off" vs. "Free guide" vs. "Anti-aging hacks newsletter"
- Test two‑step opt‑in vs. direct form

### Heatmap Analysis
- Use Hotjar or similar to track user behavior and optimize CTA placement

## 8. Performance Tracking
- Set up Google Analytics 4 to track affiliate clicks, email signups, bounce rate, time on page
- Use UTM parameters for all traffic sources
- Monitor keyword rankings weekly via SEMrush or Ahrefs
- Track affiliate commissions and ROI per channel
