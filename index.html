How to Apply It Correctly

The #1 mistake people make is using too much. Here's the right way:

1

Cleanse & Dry

Wash your face and let it dry completely. No moisturizer before application.

2

Pat, Don't Rub

Use less than a grain of rice. Gently pat onto wrinkles, bags, or pores.

3

Stay Still

Hold your face still for 2-3 minutes while it dries. Fan gently to speed up.

4

Enjoy Results

Look in the mirror. Wrinkles fade, bags disappear. Lasts up to 9 hours.

Pro Tip: Apply after your skincare routine but before makeup. If you see white residue, you used too much — wipe off and use a smaller amount next time.
